# MNEMONICS & ACRONYMS
## Memory Tricks for Principles of Law Exam - January 2026

# THE SYSTEM OF LAW

## 1. Anglo-Saxon vs Continental: "JUDGES JUDGE"
**In Anglo-Saxon law, JUDGES' decisions ARE the law (precedent)**

Remember: **"J for Judges, J for Judicial precedent"**
- Anglo-Saxon = Judges make law
- Continental = Codes make law (judges just apply)

---

## 2. Monist vs Dualist: "ONE vs TWO"
**MON**ist = **ONE** system (international + domestic together)
**DU**alist = **TWO** systems (separate)

---

## 3. Living Constitution vs Originalism: "LIVE NOW vs LOOK BACK"
- **Living** = LIVE in the NOW (current meaning)
- **Origin**alism = Look at ORIGIN (original intent)

---

## 4. Legal vs Factual Presumption: "LAW vs LOGIC"
- **Legal** presumption = Required by **LAW**
- **Factual** presumption = Based on **LOGIC**

---

## 5. Interpretation Methods: "CARE"
**C**ivil law = **C**an expand (expansive interpretation)
**A**dministrative law = **A**lways restrict (restrictive only)

---

## 6. Key Roman Principles: "PRIOR SUPERIOR RETRO"
- Lex **POSTERIOR** = Later law wins
- Lex **SUPERIOR** = Higher law wins
- Lex **RETRO** non agit = No retroactivity

---

# COPYRIGHT LAW

## 7. Moral vs Economic Rights: "MONEY ENDS, MORALS DON'T"
- **Economic** rights = Life + 70 years (MONEY eventually ENDS)
- **Moral** rights = Forever (MORALS DON'T end)

---

## 8. Moral Rights Characteristics: "PIA"
Moral rights are:
- **P**erpetual (no time limit)
- **I**nalienable (cannot be sold)
- **A**ttributable (right to be named as author)

---

## 9. What's Protected as Work: "FACE"
A work must be:
- **F**ixed (established in recognizable form)
- **A**ttributable (to a creator)
- **C**reative (original, not a copy)
- **E**xpressed (in some form - written, visual, etc.)

---

## 10. Letter Publication Consent: "RAH"
Need consent from:
- **R**ecipient (privacy)
- **A**uthor (copyright)
- **H**eirs (if author deceased)

---

## 11. Public Domain Uses: "PEEPS"
Permitted without permission:
- **P**ersonal use
- **E**ducation
- **E**xcerpts/quotations
- **P**arody
- **S**ecurity/court proceedings

---

# INDUSTRIAL PROPERTY

## 12. Duration Numbers: "20-25-10-10"
- Patent = **20** years
- Industrial Design = **25** years max
- Trademark = **10** years (renewable)
- Utility Model = **10** years

**Memory trick:** "Patent is PLENTY (20), Design is a QUARTER century (25), others are a DECADE (10)"

---

## 13. Patent Novelty: "EXPERTS EXAMINE"
Patent must be new to an **EXPERT**, not ordinary person.
**"E for Expert, E for Examine"**

---

# CARTELS

## 14. Cartel Forms: "WIDE"
Cartels can be:
- **W**ritten
- **I**nformal/Implied
- **D**ecisions of associations
- **E**xchange of information

---

## 15. Article 101 TFEU Violations: "PLMDT"
**P**rice fixing
**L**imiting production
**M**arket sharing
**D**iscrimination (dissimilar conditions)
**T**ied transactions

**Memory phrase:** "**P**eople **L**ove **M**aking **D**irty **T**rades"

---

## 16. De Minimis Thresholds: "5 Horizontal, 10 Vertical"
**"H5 - V10"**
- **H**orizontal = **5**%
- **V**ertical = **10**%

**Memory trick:** "Horizontal has HALF of Vertical"

---

## 17. Cartel vs Parallel: "COP"
- **C**artel = **C**oordinated
- **P**arallel = **O**rganic (market forces)

If there's **C**ommunication = **C**artel
If **O**nly market forces = **P**arallel

- **P**roduct market (substitutes)
- **T**erritorial market (geography)
- **T**ime framework (when)

**"PTT - Product, Territory, Time"**

---

## 19. Dominance Threshold: "40 PRESUMES"
**40%** market share = **PRESUMES** dominance (not certainty!)

---

## 20. Types of Abuse: "DRIPT"
- **D**iscrimination
- **R**efusal to deal
- **I**mposing unfair prices
- **P**redatory pricing
- **T**ied sales

---

## 21. Essential Facility Elements: "CEN"
- **C**ontrolled by dominant company
- **E**ssential for competitors
- **N**ot feasible to duplicate

- Must pay **FAIR** price

---

## 38. Profit Distribution: "SHARE not PERSON"

**"S for Share, S for Split"**

---

## 39. Liability Types: "SOUL vs SHELF"
- **SOUL** proprietorship = **U**nlimited liability (all your SOUL/assets)
- **S**hare company = **L**imited liability (only your SHares/SHELF value)
